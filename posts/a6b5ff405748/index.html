World Foundation Model Platform

就像前面所說，Cosmos是一個WFM platform，所以有兩個問題，具體來說甚麼是WFM？WFM的platform又是甚麼？

Let 𝑥0:𝑡 be a sequence of visual observations of the real world from time 0 to 𝑡. Let 𝑐𝑡 be the perturbation to the world. As illustrated in Fig. 3, a WFM is a model 𝒲 that predicts the future observation at time 𝑡 + 1, 𝑥ˆ𝑡+1, based on the past observation 𝑥0:𝑡 and the current perturbation 𝑐𝑡. In our case, 𝑥0:𝑡 is an RGB video, while 𝑐𝑡 is a perturbation that can take many forms. It can be an action taken by the Physical AI, a random perturbation, a text description of the perturbation, etc

先解釋WFM的輸入輸出，簡單來說，輸入有兩個，分別是過去的所有影像集合和一個perturbation，這個perturbation會影響下一幀的影像，輸出則是下一幀的影像，我們可以用一樣的概念來理解物理世界，過去所有的狀態資訊，再加上此事此刻發生的所有perturbation，就會產生未來的狀態，只不過，world model表示狀態資訊的格式試用影像/影片。

那我們要這個WFM和world model具體來說要做甚麼？它的目的是為了能要更好的完成policy model的policy training，其概念類似於reinforcement learning，也就是給定現在的狀況，然後給出action，也就是Policy model也會輸入影像/影片，然後會輸出一個action，這個action會造成世界的perturbation，因此policy model的輸出會做為world model的輸入，然後world model會產生下一個瞬間讓policy model來做出action，也就是說，world model的輸出會做為policy model的輸入。

因此可以想像，只要WFM做得好，就等同於policy model可以盡情地模擬與世界互動，policy model就可以被訓練得很好

•Policy evaluation. This refers to evaluating the quality of a policy model in a Physical AI system . Instead of evaluating a trained policy by deploying it to a Physical AI system operating in the real world, one could instead let the digital copy of the Physical AI system interact with the world foundation model. The WFM-based evaluation is more cost-effective and time-efficient. With the WFM, builders can deploy the policy model in unseen environments that are otherwise unavailable. WFMs can help developers rule out incapable policies quickly and focus the physical resources on a few promising ones.

•Policy initialization. A policy model generates actions to be taken by the Physical AI system based on the current observations and the given task. A well-trained WFM, which models the dynamic patterns of the world based on the input perturbations, can serve as a good initialization of the policy model. This helps address the data scarcity problem in Physical AI.

簡單來說，World model就是因為Physical AI system中的policy model的訓練很困難才出現的，理想上，我們就直接將Physical AI丟到現實環境中reinforcement learning，讓policy model慢慢學習，但這是不現實的，因此我們把這個複雜的問題切分成兩個小問題，先訓練出WFM，再用World model去訓練policy model。

前者，WFM的訓練可以完全在虛擬環境中完成，因為我們只需要給予大量現實的影片資料即可，這些資料相對好取得，後者，policy model的訓練實際上就是將world model生成的影片作為輸出，去輸出action，只要確保world model是足夠正確的，那這個也就是相對簡單的小問題。 https://ithelp.ithome.com.tw/articles/10351976 https://medium.com/change-the-world-with-technology/policy-gradient-181d43a24cf5

•Policy training. A WFM paired with a reward model can be a proxy for the physical world to provide feedback to the policy model in a reinforcement learning setup. The agent can gain proficiency in solving tasks by interacting with the WFM

換言之，WFM提供feedback，去幫助增強式訓練The agent中的policy model，拿policy model的action來當作未來會發生的幀，來train policy model。

•Planning or model-predictive control. A WFM can be used to simulate different future states following different action sequences taken by a Physical AI system. A cost/reward module can then be used to quantify the performance of these different action sequences based on the outcomes. The Physical AI can then execute the best action sequence based on the simulation results as a whole, as in planning algorithms or in a receding horizon manner, as in model-predictive control. The accuracy of the world model upper-bounds the performance of these decision-making strategies.

WFM會依據不同的action sequences產生的不同未來，換言之，policy model的表現很大程度上與world model的表現有關。

• Synthetic data generation. A WFM can be used to generate synthetic data for training. It can also be fine-tuned to be conditioned on rendering metadata such as depth or semantic maps. One can use the conditional WFM for the Sim2Real use case

Video tokenization. We develop a family of video tokenizers of different compression ratios. These tokenizers are causal. The token computation for the current frames is not based on future observation. This causal design has several benefits. On the training side, it makes joint image and video training possible since a causal video tokenizer is also an image tokenizer when the input is a single image. This is important for the video model to leverage image datasets for training, which contain rich appearance information of the worlds and tend to be more diverse. On the application side, causal video tokenizers are better aligned with Physical AI systems that live in the causal world.

WFM pre-training. We explore two scalable approaches for building pre-trained world foundation models — the diffusion model and the autoregressive model. We use the transformer architecture for its scalability

Specifically, we train the model to generate a video world based on the input text prompt. We then fine-tune it to generate a future video world based on the past video and an input text prompt, which we refer to as the Video2World generation task.

For the autoregressive-based WFM , the pre-training consists of two steps: 1) vanilla next token generation and 2) text-conditioned Video2World generation. We first train the model to generate a future video world based on the input of past video — foresight generation. We then fine-tune it to generate a future video world based on the past video and a text prompt.

Tokenizer

後面部分牽涉到比較多詳細的模型架構之類的細節我會跳過，我只著重在概念上的理解。

Tokenizers come in two types: continuous and discrete (see Fig. 7 for illustrations) .

Continuous tokenizers encode visual data into continuous latent embeddings, as in latent diffusion models like Stable Diffusion (Rombach et al., 2022) or VideoLDM (Blattmann et al., 2023) . These embeddings areD suitable for models that generate data by sampling from continuous distributions.

Discrete tokenizers encode visual data into discrete latent codes, mapping them into quantized indices, as seen in autoregressive transformers such as VideoPoet (Kondratyuk et al., 2024) . This discrete representation is necessary for models such as GPT that are trained with the cross-entropy loss. Fig. 7 illustrates the two types of tokens.

token的表示分成兩種，分別對應的就是兩種WFM，無論哪種的目標都是有效的壓縮成token然後有能力重建回影片，差異在於不同的表示方式有其優劣

We design Cosmos Tokenizer using a lightweight and computationally efficient architecture with a temporally causal mechanism. Specifically, we employ causal temporal convolution layers and causal temporal attention layers to preserve the natural temporal order of video frames, ensuring seamless tokenization of images and videos using a single unified network architecture.

有跟其他方法的tokenizer比，總之兩種的重建的品質都比較好，他們是歸功於temporally causal mechanism機制，只是這個Cosmos Tokenizer應該是同時使用Continuous跟Discrete的機制。

Our architecture employs a temporally causal design, ensuring that each stage processes only current and past frames, independent of future frames. Unlike common approaches, our tokenizer operates in the wavelet space, where inputs are first processed by a 2-level wavelet transform .

有趣的地方是這邊有提到wavelet transform，這個東西好像以前在做壓縮irradiance map看到spherical harmonics或是radial Gaussians的時候遇到，當初好像是說後者比較簡單暴力，儲存就是向量跟高斯分布參數，比較像是在空間維度上的壓縮，前者則是頻率維度上的壓縮，兩者只要參數、階數都可以重建，只不過是在效率上的不同。 https://www.chebfun.org/examples/sphere/SphericalHarmonics.html https://mynameismjp.wordpress.com/2016/10/09/sg-series-part-5-approximating-radiance-and-irradiance-with-sgs/

We use left padding of 𝑘 − 1 to ensure causality.

這是因為Temporal Convolution，就跟一般CNN的conv也會有padding，只是CNN是空間上的conv，這邊是時間上的conv

World Foundation Model Pre-training

具體來說，NV有兩種方式去train WFM，一個是diffusion-based WFM和另一個autoregressive-based WFM ， 兩者都是encode成tokens，經過transformer再decode成影片，但是前者是一次產生整段影片然後慢慢去noise，後者是一幀一幀慢慢產生影片。

這是因為diffusion-based model就是得每個tokens是連續的一段時間clip，因此其輸出的是一段影片，該頂片一開始會是雜訊，然後gradually更新成最終的clip，然後在產生下一段clip，直到產生完整的影片。相對來說，autoregressive-based model使用的tokens則是離散的，因此每次輸出都只是一張清晰的影像，然後最終產生完整的影片。

簡單來說，兩者最終都是產生一段未來的影片，換言之，兩者都是Video2World model，只是實作上不太相同，兩者雖然都是漸進的，但是diffusion-based model是在空間上漸進的更新，一次就會產生一段完整時間的影片，但是影片中每個pixel的值還沒確定，要透過迭代的方式讓整段影片逐漸變清晰，autoregressive-based model則是在時間上漸進的更新，每次產生的是完整空間的影像，每個pixel都是最終的結果，但是每次都只產生一幀，因此要透過迭代的方式，一幀一幀產生整段影片。

具體來說，前者是先訓練text2world這個model，然後再加上影片tokens輸入去finetune成video2world，後者則直接train一個video2video，輸入video然後輸出預測未來的影片，然後再加入text prompt去fine tune成video2world。

第一階段是訓練text2world，基本上架構與最後結果的video2world相同，輸入有兩個，一個是一段影片，另一個是text embedding，輸出則是tokens，差別在於，text2world中輸入的影片應該是noisy，因為現在text2world的重點在於輸出的影片也能夠與text對應，初始的影片是甚麼不重要。

訓練資料是一堆影片，這些影片會用VLM產用text prompt，在encode成embedding，這就是訓練時的輸入，同一段影片同時用tokenizer-decoder來產生tokens，這會與text2world的輸出做比較。

換言之，其實就是這兩個流程的書在計算loss

  • video- (tokenizer) ->tokens
  • video- (VLM) ->text- (text2world) ->tokens

前者就當作ground truth，經過tokenizer的tokens理論上都隱含了影片的意義，因此我們希望，text2world就算只輸入關於影片的文字描述，也要能夠直接產生類似的tokens，兩者包含了相同的意義。

因此，我們也可以注意到，text2world的training是在latent space的，因為我們不可能拿兩段影片來計算loss，因為text2world的影片不可能跟原始影片相同，我們希望的是兩段影隱含著同樣的意義，因此是使用tokens來計算loss。

Our diffusion-based WFMs are latent diffusion models that operate within a learned latent space of a tokenizer, enabling a compact, reduced-dimensional representation of videos.

等到text2world訓練完就可以把input video從noise換成真實的影片，然後text可能是要敘述正在發生的事情，然後輸出tokens，用tokens重建回影片，影片的內容應該就要是事情發生之後的畫面。

During training, our WFMs use detailed video descriptions as input text prompts to produce high-quality videos. However, during inference, user prompts may vary in length, structure, and style, often being much shorter. To bridge this gap between training and inference text prompts, we develop a prompt upsampler to transform original input prompts into more detailed and enriched versions. It can improve the prompts by adding more details and maintaining a consistent description structure, which leads to higher quality output.

從上述可以發現，訓練的時候輸入什麼樣的text很重要，所以為了確保是有效的格式與內容，所以可以用 Prompt Upsampler 來將使用者的text轉換成更詳細的prompt

Evaluation

具體的架構我這邊就不再解釋，因為技術上的東西未來可能會再演進，對我來說，更重要的是world model是怎麼評估的，更準確來說，能幫助Physical AI的world model在那些地方不能出錯，這些關乎到實際應用的前提。

Pre-trained WFMs are generalists of visual world simulation. Their capabilities should be measured across multiple aspects. Here, we evaluate our models on two aspects. First, we evaluate the 3D consistency of the generated videos. An ideal WFM should generate video simulations from geometrically plausible 3D worlds. Second, we evaluate the physics alignment of the generated videos. We calculate how well the rendered dynamics adhere to the laws of physics. Evaluation of WFMs is a highly nontrivial task. We acknowledge that there are several other important aspects required for evaluation. We leave a more comprehensive evaluation as future work.

有兩個角度去評估，3D consistency和physics alignment，換言之，可以理解這是目前他們最在乎的事情

3D Consistency

WFMs are designed to simulate 3D worlds through video generation , and it is essential to evaluate how well the generated videos are consistent with the 3D structure of the visual world. In addition to appearing realistic the generated videos should maintain coherence with the physical principles of scenes through time, a key requirement for downstream Physical AI applications.

這句話講得很白，cosmos是利用影片的形式在模擬，這邊他們認為外觀的一致性是重要的，所以cosmos基本上會先撇除掉那種外觀會變化的東西。

Metrics. Generated videos are effectively 2D projections of the underlying 3D visual worlds. We design the following metrics to measure the 3D consistency of generated videos.

1. Geometric consistency . We evaluate the 3D consistency of our generated worlds by quantifying how the epipolar geometry constraints are satisfied, including the Sampson error (Hartley and Zisserman, 2003; Sampson, 1982) and the success rate of camera pose estimation algorithms (Schönberger et al., 2016; Schönberger and Frahm, 2016) on the generated videos.

2. View synthesis consistency . We evaluate the ability of world foundation models to synthesize images at interpolated novel viewpoints while maintaining coherence with the underlying 3D structure.

幾何上的一致性用 Sampson error 檢查影片中的物件投影回3D空間後符不符合epipolar line，或者說epipolar line連不連貫，另外是 重建回camera參數的成功率 ，可以發現幾何上的東西都跟相機有關，這是因為2D影片要能夠反投影回3D空間就會與camera有關， 前者 可以單純理解成3D幾何是不是穩定的， 後者 則是把camera跟3D幾何的關係是否穩定。

合成的連貫性，簡單來說就是拿某瞬間T的結果跟T-1和T+1的結果重建出來3D場景，並且使用在T時間的相機render的結果來比對，因為3D場景是有完全的連貫性，所以可以拿來當作GT對比，因為可以撇除掉其他視覺效果的因素，所以這個算是比較純粹的影像評估，例如PSNR、SSIM、LPIPS

Physics Alignment

To explore this, we design a controlled benchmark dataset using a physics simulation engine, taking inspiration from (Kang et al., 2024) . We generate physics-grounded simulations to test the adherence of our pre-trained WFMs to Newtonian physics and rigid body dynamics . Specifically, we use simulation to generate physically correct photorealistic videos of test scenarios specific to physical laws of interest. These reference “ground truth” videos are then compared with “predicted” videos produced by a WFM given shared context (past observations and perturbation) .

簡單來說，就是拿物理跟渲染引擎去生成符合物理定律的模擬影片，然後再拿去當訓練資料，這邊模擬比較注重在牛頓定理跟剛體動力學。但是模擬終究是模擬，如果要真的能夠與現實的物理對齊還是不知道，想像中，我們只是希望WFM能夠意識到有重力、碰撞這些基本的現象，但是重力有多強這種更具體準確的參數應該是finetune的時候用真實資料去訓練的。

For evaluation, we use the following metrics:

Pixel-level metrics. For a pixel-level comparison, we compute the Peak Signal-to-Noise Ratio (PSNR) and Structural Similarity Index Measure (SSIM) to compare a predicted frame from the WFM rollout with the reference frame from the ground truth video.

Feature-level metrics. For a slightly higher-level semantic comparison, we calculate DreamSim similarity scores (Fu et al., 2023), a feature similarity metric, between the predicted and reference frames.

Object-level metrics. Finally, since we care most about how objects of interest are impacted by the ongoing physical phenomenon, we use tracking to compute object-level metrics that eliminate confounders (background changes, visual quality, etc. ) . Since the test conditions are synthetically generated, we have access to the ground-truth instance segmentation masks of the dynamic objects in the scenes. Using SAMURAI (Yang et al., 2024), we propagate the ground-truth instance masks in the first frame through the rest of the predicted video frames to extract tracks, allowing us to quantify object-level metrics. We compute the intersection-over-union (IoU) between ground truth and predicted object masks for each frame and object of interest.

總之，就是拿模擬畫面當成是GT，然後兩張圖片倆倆對比，一個最直接的方始就是逐個pixel去比較，例如常見的 PSNR、SSIM。 另一個是AIGC常用的，在特徵方面的評估 DreamSim similarity scores ，最後是物件層級的比較，具體來說就是拿SAM家族的方式把物件segment出來，然後一個計算pixel的 intersection-over-union (IoU) 。

From the table, we also find that our diffusion WFMs perform better in pixel-level prediction than our autoregressive WFMs on the 9-frame conditional setting. This correlates with our visual observation that the diffusion-based WFMs render videos with higher visual quality.

We also note that our results do not suggest that the larger model performs better on our physics alignment . While we observe larger models render videos with higher visual quality, all the WFMs equally struggle with physics adherence and require better data curation and model design.

結果上，diffusion model的視覺效果比較好，所以不意外地在 Pixel-level prediction 會表現得比較好，但是視覺效果好不代表物理表現好，這個體現在比較大的模型雖然結果漂亮，但未必會遵守物理的情況。

More generally, we observe that the rigid-body simulations described above already test the limits of our WFMs, serving as valuable tools for identifying specific failure cases. These range from low-level issues like object impermanence (spontaneous appearance and disappearance of objects) and deformation (shape changes) to more complex problems such as implausible kinematics, violation of gravity, etc.

但是本篇算是開了個頭而已，沒有預期會做到各種物理規則都能做得好，而是先專注在一些比較低階的問題，例如物體會變形之類的問題。

Temporal Sampson Error (TSE) measures whether the content generated for each camera is consistent over time. It is the median Sampson error of adjacent frames for each of the views.

Cross-view Sampson Error (CSE) measures whether multi-view consistency is preserved over time. It is the Sampson error across different generated views averaged in time. The fundamental matrix used in CSE is estimated using the keypoints accumulated across all temporal frames.

8. Related Work

World models. The concept of “world models” originated from the seminal work of Ha and Schmidhuber (2018), which proposed learning a representation of the real world using neural network models to predict future states given current states and inputs.

world model就是給定現在狀態就可以預測未來

This concept of modeling the physical world is not new; traditional automation and robotics industries have long employed mathematical models based on physics laws and system identification in planning and control algorithms.

However, these system-specific models, typically confined to low-dimensional state spaces, restrict generalization and knowledge transfer across different systems, limiting model reuse when applied to new tasks or environments

傳統的world model大多都是rule-based，只能用比較低階的參數，同時也是很難泛化的，也就是說，只要環境有一些變化，就需要重新調整參數，因為我們不能期待用簡單的規則去涵蓋複雜的變化。

Modern world model pipelines can be categorized based on their backbone architecture. Most works (Hafner et al., 2019, 2021, 2023; Hansen et al., 2024; Kim et al., 2020, 2021), including the original paper (Ha and Schmidhuber, 2018) by Ha and Schmidhuber, employ a recurrent neural network to model system state evolution in a latent space learned via an autoencoder.

More recent trends view world models as generative models in visual observation space , often in the form of conditional video generative models (e.g., action-tovideo, text-to-video) . These models can be either autoregressive (Bruce et al., 2024; Liu et al., 2024; Micheli et al., 2023; Robine et al., 2023; Yang et al., 2023) or diffusion-based (Alonso et al., 2024; Ding et al., 2024; Valevski et al., 2024), as considered in this work. Another promising approach is generative simulation (Hua et al., 2024; Nasiriany et al., 2024), which combines generative AI and physical simulators to model the real world.

比較現代的方式大概分成三種，autoencoder在高階的latent space學習狀態變化、conditional video generative models和generative simulation

A well-trained world model can be applied in various ways, including verification (Hu et al., 2023), planningbased model predictive control (Bar et al., 2024; Hansen et al., 2024), and model-based reinforcement learning (Alonso et al., 2024; Ding et al., 2024; Robine et al., 2023; Yang et al., 2023; Zhang et al., 2024) .

無論是哪一種對於都可以有很多延伸的應用，很顯然，cosmos是general的world model，用的方法是 video generative models。

Diffusion-based models could render videos with better visual quality. Autoregressive-based models can better leverage all sorts of techniques developed by the LLM community. We build both diffusion-based (Cosmos-Diffusion) and autoregressive-based (Cosmos-Autoregressive) WFMs and make them available to the Physical AI builders.

在生成式運動規劃（Generative Motion Planning）中，系統通常會先生成中間的視覺子目標（如圖像或影片），然後透過其他模型將這些視覺子目標轉換為具體的動作序列，以控制機器人完成任務。這種方法的優勢在於，視覺子目標能夠在不同環境中泛化，使機器人能夠適應多變的環境。

總之，生成式運動規劃透過生成中間的視覺子目標，並結合運動控制模型或階層式控制架構，將這些目標轉化為具體的動作序列，以實現對機器人的精確控制。

Conclusions and Discussions

Limitations . Despite the progress, the development of world foundation models is still in the early stages. Current models, including ours, fall short as reliable simulators of the physical world. We observe that our models still suffer from issues, including the lack of object permanence, inaccuracies in contact-rich dynamics, and inconsistency in instruction following. Additionally, the realism of the generated videos does not always reflect adherence to fundamental physical principles, such as gravity, light interactions, and fluid dynamics.

這邊也承認目前還是早期，很難真的模擬出現實世界

Autoregressive vs. Diffusion WFMs. Our evaluation results in 3D consistency (Sec. 5.3.1) and video generation for robotics (Sec. 6.2) indicate that diffusion-based WFMs currently deliver better generation quality.

However, autoregressive-based WFMs possess significant untapped potential. They could (1) leverage pre-trained weights from large language models (LLMs) to inherit extensive world knowledge and (2) enable faster generation through the use of advanced inference optimization techniques designed for causal attention.

但是autoregressive比較容易與其他現有的GENAI去做合作甚至繼承，而且能夠快速產生下一幀，適合realtime的應用，例如前面提到的SuSIE那種要放到邊緣裝置的model。

Recent advancements have shown that diffusion transformers with bidirectional attention can be distilled into student transformers with causal attention, enabling support for key-value caching during inference (Yin et al., 2024) . Similarly, autoregressive models can incorporate locally bidirectional attention to generate images via diffusion heads (Zhou et al., 2024) .

以下是我當初自己對於一些名詞的理解，也才有了 【Cosmos】Physical AI, Agent and World Model 這篇

  • Physical AI is an AI system equipped with sensors and actuators that can observe, interact with, and modify the world.
  • The agent is a component of the Physical AI system which interacts with the world.
  • A world model is a digital twin of the physical world that the Physical AI can safely interact with [1–3] . It learns a representation of the real world using neural network models to predict future states given current states and inputs [2–4] .
  • A world foundation model (WFM) is a general-purpose world model that can be fine-tuned into customized world models for downstream applications [3, 5, 6] . Pre-trained WFMs are trained with large-scale, diverse video datasets capturing different aspects of real-world physics, so they can become generalists [2, 3, 7] .
  • A policy model generates actions to be taken by the Physical AI system (or agent) based on the current observations and the given task [2, 3, 8] . It can be initialized or trained using a world model [2, 3, 8] .

In summary, Physical AI systems use policy models to act in the world World models, especially world foundation models, help the agent understand and predict the world’s behavior, enabling better policy model training and decision-making.

簡單來說，world model是現實世界的虛擬分身，你只要給動作(prompt)，就會與世界互動，就會影響世界產生下一秒新的世界，具體來說，就是會產生下一幀的畫面，可以想像，如果你有了一個很好的world model，那你就可以在裡面train policy model，無論是用合成資料train還是reinforcement learning，然後加上現實的agent(機器人)構成的system稱之為Physical AI。

WFM 是大公司才train得起來的超大general world model，通常拿來當作pre-train model，當然也可當作world model使用。
